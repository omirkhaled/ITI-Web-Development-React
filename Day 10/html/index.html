Our Story

From one roaster in a garage to a name Egypt trusts.

It started twenty years ago with a single roaster, a stubborn belief that coffee deserved better, and a lot of trial and error. Today we're proud members of The Specialty Coffee Association, working directly with farms big and small — from single-origin micro-lots to full estate partnerships.

What hasn't changed is the process: every batch is still tasted, tracked from origin to cup, and roasted by hand. It's slower this way. It's also the only way we know how to do it right.
